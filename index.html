Did you know

In 2017, an estimated 1 in 8 Americans were food insecure, equating to 40 million Americans including more than 12 million children.

Children who experience food insecurity are at a higher risk of developing asthma, struggling with anxiety or depression, and performing poorly in school or physical activities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to feed the hungry by eliminating restaurant food waste. This empowers food establishments to support non profit organizations that aid people in need. Through this partnership we work to reduce food waste and help sustain people in need.
