Our Mission

Replate connects Restaurant owners, volunteer drivers, participating non profits, and organizations that work with people in need to solve food insecurity.

We make it easy to learn how much food is available at any given restaurant so that a volunteer driver can deliver the food to places with people who need it.

Everyday People Solving Hunger

Every day ordinary people like restuarant employees, volunteers, and shelter workers connect to deliver tens of thousands of plates to food insecure families around the country.

Our efforts to connect generous helpers aim to decrease the number of people, including 40 million Americans and more than 12 million children, who experience food insecurity.

How You Can Help

Since 1 in 8 Americans experience food insecurity, chances are that you or someone close to you has been affected by this dehibilitating issue. At Replate, we love working with compassionate, action-driven partners to make a dent in a solvable crisis.

Restaurants

If you're associated with a Restaurant that commonly throws good food away, joining Replate can help you turn throw-away trash into life-saving meals. Our app helps you communicate with volunteer drivers.

Volunteers

Replate Volunteers help transport food from participating restaurants to shelters and non profits that work with food insecure families. Our app helps you know exactly when, where, and how to make a successful food delivery.

Non Profits

Many non profit organizations work with food insecure families. Our Replate app helps connect you with Restaurants and Volunteers ready to help. Expand your impact by feeding more families!
